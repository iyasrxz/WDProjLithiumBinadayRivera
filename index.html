The Fire Gem - Ang Brilyante ng Apoy

After the splitting of the mother gem, the Fire Gem was entrusted to Hathorians lead by haring Arvak and his son, Hagorn. The hathors, like fire, are determined and driven. They aren't easily stopped. The fire gem gives the ability to control fire, shapeshift, etc.

The Air Gem - Ang Brilyante ng Hangin

The Air Gem was entrusted to Lireo. Like air, they are very adaptable. Lireo is the most advanced kingdom in terms of technology and infrastructure, thus the air gem is very fitting for them. The air gem gives the ability to control air and simulate flight.

The Water Gem - Ang Brilyante ng Tubig

The Water Gem was entrusted to Adamya, a kingdom of unique beings. Like water, they are calm, kind, and free-spirited. The water gem gives the ability to control water, special encantations, etc.

The Earth Gem - Ang Brilyante ng Lupa

The Earth Gem was entrusted to Sapiro, a kingdom of strong and resilient warriors. Like earth, they are grounded and stable. The earth gem gives the ability to control the land, shapeshift into animals, etc.
