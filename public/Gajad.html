GAJAD

Emre

Bathala ng Lahat | Supreme Deity

Emre is the most powerful god in Encantadia—the creator of worlds and all living beings. He is the source of the power of the four Gems and the true keeper of balance. Silent and not easily swayed to intervene, he acts only when there is a great threat to the overall order. He grants blessings and punishment according to the destiny and worthiness of every being.

Arde

Bathala ng Kadiliman | Diyos na Naghimagsik

Arde is the brother of Emre who was once equal in power but was consumed by envy and the desire to dominate. For attempting to seize the throne of the gods, he was marked as the deity who brings darkness and chaos. He is the source of evil creatures and malevolent forces in Encantadia, and he remains a constant threat to the peace and balance of the world.

Ether

Bathaluman ng Digmaan at Karunungan

Ether is a powerful goddess known for her intelligence, courage, and immense influence over the course of war. She is the source of the Ethereals’ strength and often uses strategy to manipulate events according to her will. Clever, inventive, and deeply knowledgeable about power and destiny.

Keros

Bathala ng Apoy | Diyos ng Lakas at Katapangan

Keros is the god who embodies fire, strength, and courage. He blesses warriors with steadfast spirits and fierce determination. Though fierce and hot-headed, he carries an honorable heart. He is also recognized as one of the powerful guardians of Encantadia against the forces of evil.

Haliya

Bathaluman ng Buwan at Liwanag

Haliya is a loving and compassionate goddess who brings light in the midst of darkness. She is the protector of travelers and those in need of guidance. She is known for her gentle yet powerful presence, often symbolizing hope, clarity, and protection.

Cassiopea

Unang Reyna ng Lireo | Bathaluman ng Propesiya

Cassiopea is one of the most renowned fairies and goddesses in Encantadia. As the first queen of Lireo and a champion of peace, she divided the Mother Gem into five to preserve balance. She is known as the Goddess of Prophecy because of her ability to see the future. Wise, just, and always willing to make sacrifices for the world.

Pirena

Pirena is the fiery eldest Sang’gre, wielder of the Brilyante ng Apoy. She is the daughter of the Lirean Queen Minea with the Hathor King Hagorn. Fiercely competitive and passionate, she struggles between ambition and family loyalty.

Amihan

Amihan is the calm and thoughtful Sang’gre of Hangin. She was born in the Kingdom of Lireo during the war between Sapiro and Hathoria, as the second daughter of the Lirean Queen Minea with the only daughter of Rehav Raquim of Sapiro. As queen, she is known for her wisdom, fairness, and strength in protecting Lireo from war. She became the mother of Lira and the lover of Rama Ybrahim.

Alena

Alena is the gentle and enchanting Sang’gre of Tubig. She is the third daughter of Hara Minea with Enuo's first child. Gifted with a magical singing voice, she is compassionate yet incredibly strong when defending those she loves.

Danaya

Danaya is the fierce and loyal Sang’gre of Lupa. She is the fourth and youngest daughter of Queen Minea of Lireo with Enuo's second child. Honorable, straightforward, and deeply principled, she strongly upholds the traditions of Lireo.

Minea

Minea is the mother of the Sang’gres and the revered Queen of Lireo before Amihan. Strong, principled, and protective, she embodies leadership founded on justice and love for her people.

Hagorn

Hagorn is the main antagonist of Encantadia 2016 series. He is the son of Rama Arvak and became the third king of Hathoria. He claimed to be the King of all Encantadia after the successful conquest of Lireo. He is the father of Sang'gre Pirena and Diwani Deshna. He was once a good man, but became bitter when he lost his betrothed, Minea, to the throne of Lireo.

Raquim

Raquim is a prince of Sapiro, a cousin to the king, Armeo. His rank appeared to be second only to the king, as the nearest collateral relative.

Enuo

Enuo is a Sapiryan who rescued infant Amihan from Gurna. Despite not seeing his daughters, Alena and Danaya, for a very long time, he has deep affections for them. He will do everything in his capacity to help his daughters in times of need.

Gamil

Gamil is a diwata soldier of Lireo and the father of Mira. was apologetic to Alira Naswen when he was unwillingly forced by Ether to reveal Cassiopea's whereabouts in order to save his own soul.

Mira

Mira is the first daughter of Pirena, raised in Lireo as royalty. Kind yet strong-willed, she embodies compassion while carrying the fiery spirit of her lineage.

Azulan

High Chieftain Azulan of Punjabwe, is a loyal subordinate of Rehav Manik of Punjabwe before joining forces with Lireo. He is the brother of Ariana, the chosen keeper of the Air Gem due to being Amihan’s reincarnation. He later marries Pirena and they have their daughter Flamarra, thus turning into King Consort of Hathoria and Mira's stepfather.

Ybrahim/Ybarro

Rama Ybrahim of Sapiro, also known as Ybarro, was the lost heir to the throne of Sapiro. Ybrahim is a protective father to Lira, his daughter from Hara Amihan, and to Kahlil, his son from Sang'gre Alena. He acceded as the fourth Rama of Sapiro upon the restoration of peace in Encantadia.

Lira

Lira is the daughter of Amihan and the prophesied savior of Encantadia. Brave, curious, and big-hearted, she learns the meaning of leadership throughout her journey.

Kahlil

Kahlil is the son of Rama Ybrahim of Sapiro and Sang'gre Alena of Lireo. He is the younger half-brother of Lira. He is the first known male of the Royal House of Lireo. Due to the circumstances of his birth, his growth was accelerated by his mother's spell to enable him to survive on his own.

Memfes

Memfes is the chief leader of the Gunikars, a tribe near the land of Adamya.

Aquil

Aquil was the former general of the Diwata army in Lireo, and the love interest of Danaya. He eventually married Danaya and they have their daughter Gaiea.

Gaiea

Gaiea was the first daughter of Danaya and Aquil. She was raised alongside her cousins, Flamarra, Adamus, and Cassandra. She was trained in magic and combat by Danaya at a young age.

Adamus

Adamus is Alena's second son, and the only son of the late Chief Memfes of Gunikar tribe. He is part of the next generation of Sang'gres. He is the next Keeper of the Water Gem.

Flamarra

Flamarra is the second daughter of Pirena, and the first daughter of Pirena's husband, Azulan. She is part of the next generation of Sang'gres, and the next Keeper of the Fire Gem.

Cassandra

Cassandra is the daughter of Lira. Her creation is unique, due to being made from a lock of Lira's hair, blood of Bathalumang Cassiopea, and ashes of Hara Amihan.

Agane

Agane is Hagorn’s fierce right-hand assassin; a single glance from her is enough to strike fear into her enemies. Ambitious and driven, Agane came from the lowest ranks of the Hathorian military but persevered until she earned Hagorn’s recognition and trust.

Gurna

Gurna is the Dama of Sang’gre Pirena. Gurna is a manipulative spy of Hathoria within the kingdom of Lireo. She influences Pirena’s thinking in order to achieve Hathoria’s goal of ruling Encantadia.

Imaw

Imaw serves as the chief adviser to the fairies. His weapon is the Mahiwagang Tungkod, a magical staff that can reveal the history of almost anything.

Banak and Nakba

Banak and Nakba reside in the kingdom of Adamya. They always fight side by side and usually act as bait to trap the Hathors.

Wantuk

Wantuk is the witty and lucky thief who is friends with Ybarro and Pako. He is good at putting on a brave front but is the first to retreat from a fight. He prefers battles that do not involve weapons.

Paopao

Paopao was a young boy from the human world who had been abducted by the bandidos of Encantadia. He is revealed to be the first keeper of the Brilyante ng Diwa, which was a shard of the Mother Gem.
